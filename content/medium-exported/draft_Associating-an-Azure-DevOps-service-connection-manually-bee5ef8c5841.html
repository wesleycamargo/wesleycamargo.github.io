Associating an Azure DevOps service connection manually

Open App Registration in your Active Directory menu and click on New registration. Then name your service connection and click in Register

After creation, click on Certificates & secrets and New client secret

Give a name to your secret and click in add:

Copy the value generated. It will not be available for copy again. If you need it later, it will be necessary to generate a new secret.
