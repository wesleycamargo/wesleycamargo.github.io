What is Azure VM Auto-Shutdown?

Azure VM Auto-Shutdown is like setting a timer for your Azure VM. Auto-shutdown allows you to set a specific time so your Virtual Machine will turn off. This is a handful when you create VMs for tests, or even in development/QA environments, where you don‚Äôt need your VMs running at night when your team is not working.
